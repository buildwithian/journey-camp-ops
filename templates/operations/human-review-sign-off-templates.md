# Human Review Sign-off Templates

Use this guide with the native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1devu3dd7yzkx047MJ1QWDiVmOcy2vcrHy7nmxvwSNlE/edit

This package prepares decision capture for HR-001 through HR-007. It does not assign reviewers, approve any review gate, close a launch gate, authorize restricted-data replacement, approve website access or authorize publication.

## When To Use

Use the sign-off templates only after a qualified reviewer has received the relevant HR review packet and completed an actual review.

Record final outcomes in the live `Human Review` tab only when the reviewer identity, authority basis, decision, decision date, conditions or required changes, and evidence link are known.

## Shared Record Fields

- HR ID
- Review area
- Reviewer role
- Reviewer name or authorized identifier
- Reviewer authority basis
- Decision: Pending / Approved / Approved with conditions / Changes required / Not approved
- Decision date
- Conditions or required changes
- Evidence link or notes
- Linked tracker update completed by

## Gate Templates

### HR-001 Safeguarding Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review safeguarding and child-protection readiness for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-001 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture safeguarding policy readiness, escalation path, incident response owner, adult leader responsibilities and restricted-data handling.

### HR-002 Local Law Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review local legal compliance for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-002 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture country-specific legal requirements, consent expectations, venue or transport obligations, data and privacy obligations and escalation owner.

### HR-003 Medical Process Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review medical readiness and medication processes for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-003 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture emergency procedure, medication handling, information minimization, incident recording boundary and responsible adult role.

### HR-004 Theology Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review theological and biblical content for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-004 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture theological alignment, teaching boundaries, leader guidance, content that needs revision and approved source boundaries.

### HR-005 Operating Authority Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review final decision rights and operating authority for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-005 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture final authority owner, escalation path, delegated responsibility boundaries, backup coverage and unresolved governance conflicts.

### HR-006 Girls Programme Content Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review girls programme content and rite-of-passage content for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-006 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture age and context suitability, pastoral sensitivity, leader guidance, parent or guardian communication needs and content restrictions.

### HR-007 Copyright Boundary Sign-off

Reviewer statement: I confirm that I am authorized to review third-party benchmark and copyright boundaries for The Journey V3. I have reviewed the HR-007 packet and record my decision in the Human Review register.

Capture materials that can be used, materials requiring permission, materials to remove, attribution expectations and website publication limits.

## Tracker Update Rule

After real review is complete, update `Human Review` first. Update `Gate Evidence Checklist` and `Launch Gates` only when all required proof exists. Do not mark a launch gate complete from this template alone.
