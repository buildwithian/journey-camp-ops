# Leadership Review Schedule

## Purpose

Use this schedule to turn open gate evidence into real leadership review sessions with dates, facilitator roles, attendee roles, preparation links, expected outputs and completion proof.

## Current Live-state Note

The live `Leadership Review Schedule` tab is populated with the nine schedule rows from [leadership-review-schedule.csv](leadership-review-schedule.csv), linked into Dashboard row 19, Instructions row 19, Launch Gates evidence fields and Resource Library Index quality checks. Keep the CSV as the recovery/import template.

## Privacy Rule

Record roles, dates, agenda outputs and links only. Do not enter participant data, medical details, financial account data, private contact details or source-derived names in this schedule.

## Status Values

- Needs date
- Needs facilitator
- Needs attendees confirmed
- Scheduled
- Prep incomplete
- Ready
- Needs pilot run
- Completed - outputs pending
- Outputs recorded
- Blocked

## Operating Sequence

1. Start with SCHED-001 to align the team on gates, evidence and privacy boundaries.
2. Schedule owner, governance, reviewer-assignment, pilot, data, access and publication sessions in dependency order.
3. Update the listed target tracker ranges only after the relevant meeting output is recorded by the accountable human owner.
4. Keep production publishing blocked until access, authority, content-review and copyright evidence is complete.

## Local Import Template

The CSV template contains nine schedule rows:

- SCHED-001 for launch gate orientation and evidence mapping.
- SCHED-002 through SCHED-003 for owner appointment and governance authority review.
- SCHED-004 for HR-001 through HR-007 reviewer assignment.
- SCHED-005 through SCHED-006 for pilot planning, run debrief and change routing.
- SCHED-007 for restricted real-data replacement review.
- SCHED-008 for website access model decision.
- SCHED-009 for production publication go/no-go review.
