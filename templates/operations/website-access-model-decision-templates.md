# Website Access Model Decision Templates

Use this guide with the native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1YToT3HxWpQFXbe2npBRpKX75cAIwZ39zrgbu6bE1ZJc/edit

This package prepares access-model decision capture for GATE-005 and DEC-004. It does not approve access, broaden sharing, authorize authenticated tools, unblock production website work or publish production pages by itself.

## When To Use

Use the decision templates with `Website Access Review!A5:J18` before any production website is built, deployed, shared more broadly or connected to real operational data.

Choose one overall access model and record area-level decisions for WAR-001 through WAR-014. Keep the prototype separate from production until DEC-004, DEC-006, TASK-015, TASK-016 and required HR gates show accepted evidence.

## Overall Access Model Decision Record

- Decision ID
- Selected model: leaders-only / partly public with protected leader area / public information plus authenticated operational tools
- Approver role
- Approver name or authorized identifier
- Authority basis
- Decision date
- Public areas approved
- Protected leader areas approved
- Restricted authenticated areas approved
- Withheld or deferred areas
- Drive source-of-truth boundary
- Content owner approval status
- Data-owner approval status
- Copyright boundary status
- Operating authority status
- Conditions or limits
- Affected Website Access Review rows
- DEC-004 update completed by
- Publication impact: still blocked / ready for publication gate review / not approved

## Area-Level Decision Record

- Website Access Review item ID
- Website area
- Recommended minimum access
- Final access decision: public / protected leader area / restricted authenticated only / withheld / deferred
- Reason for decision
- What content may be shown
- What content must stay out
- Source-of-truth Drive or Sheet location
- Owner role
- Required approver role
- Approver name or authorized identifier
- Approval dependency status
- Conditions or required changes
- Decision date
- Evidence link or notes
- Website backlog impact

## Access Model Wording Blocks

### Leaders-Only

The Journey website and operating hub remain available only to approved leaders. No public pages, public forms, public resources, public media or public schedules are approved by this decision. Production publication remains blocked until the publication gate is separately approved.

### Partly Public With Protected Leader Area

Approved public pages may explain The Journey and provide approved public dates or interest pathways. Leader resources, dashboards, assignments, review evidence, trackers, participant administration, medical, finance, safeguarding, pastoral and restricted materials remain protected or restricted as recorded in Website Access Review.

### Public Information Plus Authenticated Operational Tools

Approved public information may be published separately from authenticated operational tools. Any tool that stores or displays real local data must have data-owner approval, access controls and source-of-truth ownership recorded before use.

### Not Approved Or Deferred

The website access model is not ready for production. Keep the prototype as a review artifact, keep Drive and Sheets as the source of truth, and record the missing authority, content, copyright, data or access decision before revisiting publication.

## Publication Gate Handoff

Before production publication can be considered, confirm:

- DEC-004 records the selected access model and area decisions.
- DEC-006 remains blocked until publication authority approves it.
- TASK-015 is complete and TASK-016 is authorized by the approved accountable authority.
- HR-005 operating authority and HR-007 copyright boundaries are accepted or conditions are resolved.
- Restricted data is absent from public pages, website exports, screenshots, shared templates and unapproved tools.
- The production commit, URL, approver, date and conditions are recorded before deployment.

## Tracker Update Rule

Update Website Access Review first, then DEC-004. Update Launch Gates only when all GATE-005 proof exists. Do not start production publishing or mark GATE-005 complete from this template alone.
