# Restricted Data Placement Approval Templates

Use this guide with the native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1uDcHCA-BgeQb44zjpqt52kQx83cVGNDiVFXoRmLT8HU/edit

This package prepares approval capture for GATE-004 and DEC-005. It does not authorize collection, disclosure, storage, migration, sharing, publication or deletion of restricted data by itself.

## When To Use

Use the placement approval templates after a synthetic example or placeholder has been identified and before any real local data is entered into Drive, Sheets, Docs, website prototypes, exports, screenshots or production tools.

Do not paste real restricted data into this template. Record the approval decision, category, storage location, access group, field purpose and retention boundary instead.

## Approval Boundary

- Approval must name the exact storage location or approved system.
- Approval must name the exact access group or role boundary.
- Approval must explain why each field is minimum necessary for the workflow.
- Approval must define retention or review expectation.
- Approval must identify duplicate-copy handling.
- Approval must confirm whether the data is barred from shared trackers, website pages, exports, screenshots or public materials.
- When any item is unclear, leave the example synthetic and log the question as a data restriction.

## Placement Approval Record Fields

- Approval ID
- Requested replacement
- Data category: participant / family or guardian / leader / medical / pastoral / financial / reviewer / venue or security / media or copyright / other
- Synthetic example or placeholder location
- Minimum necessary field list
- Workflow purpose
- Proposed source of truth
- Approved storage location or system
- Approved access group
- Shared tracker boundary
- Website or publication boundary
- Retention or review expectation
- Duplicate-copy handling
- Migration allowed: yes / no / only with conditions
- Approver role
- Approver name or authorized identifier
- Approval status: pending / approved / approved with conditions / changes required / not approved
- Decision date
- Conditions or required changes
- Evidence link or notes
- Linked tracker update completed by

## Decision Wording Blocks

### Approved

I confirm that the requested replacement may use the approved storage location, access group, field list and retention expectation recorded here. Only the approved minimum necessary data may be entered, and broad shared trackers may hold status or category notes only.

### Approved With Conditions

I confirm that the requested replacement may proceed only after the conditions listed here are complete. Until then, the example remains synthetic and no restricted data should be entered into broad shared locations.

### Changes Required

I cannot approve the requested replacement as written. Revise the field list, storage location, access group, retention expectation or duplicate-copy handling before any restricted data is entered.

### Not Approved

The requested replacement is not approved. Keep the example synthetic, remove any unapproved restricted data from broad locations through an authorized process, and record the follow-up action in the correct tracker.

## Category-Specific Prompts

- Participant or family data: Which fields are essential, where is consent or authority recorded, and which broad trackers must remain role-only or aggregate-only?
- Medical or emergency data: Which qualified medical or safety owner approves the location, and how will emergency access be limited to the right people?
- Pastoral data: Which pastoral authority approves storage, who can see it, and what must never be copied into shared debriefs or dashboards?
- Financial data: Which finance owner approves storage, what personal payment details are excluded, and how are aggregate budget records separated from private records?
- Leader or reviewer data: Which contact details stay out of shared trackers, and what approved identifier can be used instead?
- Venue, logistics or security data: Which details can be shared broadly, and which route plans, security notes or supplier details need restricted access?
- Media or copyright data: Which consent, permission or license record exists before any image, video, screenshot, excerpt or branded asset is used?
- Website or publication data: Which content is public, leader-only, authenticated, restricted or withheld before production publishing?

## Tracker Update Rule

Update DEC-005 or TASK-014 only after a real accountable data owner records an approval decision. Update Resource Library quality checks or Launch Gates only when accepted evidence proves the approved location, access group, field purpose and retention expectation. Do not mark GATE-004 complete from this template alone.
