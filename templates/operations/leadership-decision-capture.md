# Leadership Decision Capture

## Purpose

Use this register during authorized leadership review sessions to capture the actual decisions that move Journey V3 from prepared operating system into real implementation. It gives leaders one place to enter real owner names, reviewer outcomes, pilot evidence, restricted-data placement approvals, website access decisions, content approval evidence and publication go/no-go decisions.

## Current Live-state Note

The live `Leadership Decision Capture` tab is populated with the 18 rows from [leadership-decision-capture.csv](leadership-decision-capture.csv), linked into Dashboard row 24, Instructions row 32 and Resource Library Index quality checks. Keep the CSV as the recovery/import template.

## Privacy Rule

Record names, roles, decisions, dates, evidence links and conditions only when the accountable authority has approved the entry. Do not store private contact details, participant records, medical information, banking details, private family data, restricted review evidence or source-derived names in this shared tab.

## Status Values

- Needs owner decision
- Needs authority approval
- Needs reviewer decision
- Needs pilot run
- Needs debrief
- Needs data owner approval
- Needs access decision
- Needs content approval
- Needs publication approval
- In review
- Changes required
- Accepted / complete
- Not approved / withheld
- Blocked until prerequisites accepted

## Operating Sequence

1. Use this tab during the leadership session after START HERE, the Launch Gate Review Pack and the relevant packets are open.
2. Keep `Name or decision to enter`, `Decision date`, `Evidence link` and `Conditions / changes` blank until the accountable human owner records evidence.
3. Update the listed target tracker only after the decision row has real authority, date and evidence.
4. Close a launch gate only when the corresponding decision-capture rows and gate-evidence rows prove completion.
5. Keep production website work blocked until access, authority, copyright, content, restricted-data and publication rows are accepted.

## Local Import Template

The CSV template contains 18 capture rows:

- LDC-001 through LDC-003 for owner appointment and governance authority.
- LDC-004 through LDC-010 for HR-001 through HR-007.
- LDC-011 through LDC-012 for real pilot scheduling, debrief and accepted changes.
- LDC-013 through LDC-014 for restricted-data placement and real local information entry.
- LDC-015 for website access model approval.
- LDC-016 for website content approval evidence.
- LDC-017 through LDC-018 for publication go/no-go and production build handoff.

## Non-Approval Boundary

This register does not appoint leaders, approve HR gates, run a pilot, migrate restricted data, approve website access, approve website content, authorize Alpha asset copying, build, deploy, publish pages, broaden sharing or close launch gates. It records accountable human decisions after they are made.
