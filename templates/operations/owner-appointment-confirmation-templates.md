# Owner Appointment Confirmation Templates

Use this guide with the native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1Q3-cpnyY8hh2ov4hvlcNu1Y0lvgC1ZkTDuLelybrTrI/edit

This package prepares appointment decision capture for APP-001 through APP-012. It does not nominate candidates, appoint leaders, approve authority, update `Leader Roster`, close GATE-001, authorize restricted-data access, approve website access or authorize publication.

## When To Use

Use the confirmation templates only after programme owners or authorized governance reviewers have nominated a real candidate and confirmed the required approval source.

Record final outcomes in the live `Owner Appointment Routing` and `Leader Roster` tabs only when the candidate, backup coverage, authority basis, availability, training status, access group and evidence link are known.

## Shared Appointment Record Fields

- Appointment ID
- Owner role
- Candidate leader name or approved identifier
- Backup role
- Backup leader confirmed
- Approval source
- Authority basis
- Appointment decision: Pending / Confirmed / Confirmed with conditions / Changes required / Not appointed
- Decision date
- Availability and training status
- Access group
- Evidence link or notes
- Leader Roster update completed by

## Role Templates

### APP-001 Journey Lead Confirmation

Confirm nomination authority, backup coverage, availability, training status, launch-gate ownership and unresolved launch-gate conditions before roster entry.

### APP-002 Oversight Lead Confirmation

Confirm governance authority, escalation ownership, access-model recommendation responsibility, HR-005 dependency and publication boundary.

### APP-003 Administration Lead Confirmation

Confirm participant administration responsibility, approved data-owner dependency, legal/privacy requirements, restricted storage rule and fields that must stay out of shared tools.

### APP-004 Logistics Lead Confirmation

Confirm venue, equipment, transport and setup responsibility within approved safety and event-readiness boundaries.

### APP-005 Session Coordinator Confirmation

Confirm leader training, weekly-session execution and pilot-feedback responsibility after content, theology and programme-fidelity dependencies are understood.

### APP-006 Camp Director Confirmation

Confirm kickoff, Compass and rite-of-passage logistics responsibility within approved safeguarding, medical, legal and safety boundaries.

### APP-007 Youth Track Lead Confirmation

Confirm boys and girls track adaptation responsibility within approved programme-content and theology boundaries.

### APP-008 Pastoral Lead Confirmation

Confirm theology review preparation responsibility and whether the role has authority to approve theology or must route approval elsewhere.

### APP-009 Safety Lead Confirmation

Confirm safeguarding, medical, legal and activity-safety routing responsibility after qualified reviewers record required outcomes.

### APP-010 Finance Lead Confirmation

Confirm budget, payment and reimbursement control responsibility within approved restricted-data and finance-authority boundaries.

### APP-011 Communications Lead Confirmation

Confirm public/protected communication, media and website-content responsibility after access-model and copyright approvals are recorded.

### APP-012 Knowledge Management Lead Confirmation

Confirm Drive source-of-truth, resource-index and template recovery responsibility within approved access, data and publication boundaries.

## Tracker Update Rule

After real appointment approval is complete, update `Owner Appointment Routing` first, then `Leader Roster`. Update `Governance Authority Review`, `Decisions` and `Launch Gates` only when the required authority proof exists. Do not mark GATE-001 complete from this template alone.
