# Website Content Approval Templates

Use this guide with the native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1RgevIzRPXYjB1cJkECdt5R2szmOSp6oAZ_6a_vhOKYk/edit

This package supports content-owner, copyright, restricted-data and publication-handoff review for proposed website content. It does not approve content, approve access, approve copyright use, authorize Alpha asset copying, broaden sharing, deploy a production website, publish pages or close GATE-006 by itself.

## When To Use

Use these templates with `Website Content Review!A5:N24` before any WCR row is marked approved, ready for access-model review or ready for publication. The templates capture decision evidence for WCR-001 through WCR-020 while keeping Drive and the live Sheets as the source of truth.

Do not copy Alpha assets, wording, screenshots, media, branded design elements, course content or third-party resources unless explicit permission and usage terms are recorded. When permission is absent or unclear, create an original Journey summary or keep the item withheld.

## Template 1: Content Scope Approval

- Content approval ID
- Related Website Content Review row ID
- Website area or page
- Source-of-truth Drive or Sheet location
- Proposed public-safe summary
- Proposed protected leader-only summary
- Content owner role
- Content owner name or authorized identifier
- Review type required
- Decision: not approved / approved for summary only / approved for protected leader area / withheld / deferred
- Decision date
- Conditions, edits or exclusions
- Evidence location

### Decision Wording

The named content owner has reviewed the proposed scope for this website item. The approved scope is limited to the areas, wording level, access treatment and conditions recorded here. Any expanded content, new media, restricted data, external quotation, theological claim, programme change or access change requires a new approval record.

## Template 2: Public-Safe Summary Approval

- Related WCR row ID
- Public-facing purpose
- Approved plain-language summary
- Details explicitly excluded from public view
- Date or season wording allowed
- Call-to-action wording allowed
- Reviewer role
- Reviewer name or authorized identifier
- Decision date
- Conditions

### Decision Wording

The approved public summary may be used only as a high-level description. It must not include participant records, leader private contact details, family information, medical information, banking information, safeguarding details, unapproved event logistics, unapproved theology, restricted schedules or private Drive links.

## Template 3: Protected Or Restricted Handling Approval

- Related WCR row ID
- Material category
- Minimum access level required
- Reason for protection or restriction
- Data owner role
- Data owner name or authorized identifier
- Allowed users or roles
- Storage and source-of-truth location
- Website handling: show summary / show link / withhold / authenticated tool only
- Decision date
- Conditions

### Decision Wording

This material is not approved for public pages. It may be referenced only within the access level recorded here and only while Drive or the approved live Sheet remains the source of truth. Restricted data must not be exported, copied into static pages, embedded in screenshots or exposed through unapproved tools.

## Template 4: Media, Video And Copyright Approval

- Related WCR row ID
- Media item or source
- Rights holder or source owner
- Permission basis
- Usage allowed: link only / embed / screenshot / quote / original summary / not allowed
- Expiry or renewal requirement
- Credit wording required
- Copyright reviewer role
- Copyright reviewer name or authorized identifier
- Decision date
- Conditions

### Decision Wording

The media or third-party material may be used only in the form recorded here. Do not copy Alpha assets unless explicit permission and usage terms are recorded. When permission is absent or unclear, use an original Journey summary, a non-public reference for leaders or a placeholder until approval is obtained.

## Template 5: Date And Schedule Publishing Approval

- Related WCR row ID
- Date or schedule item
- Public wording approved
- Protected leader-only details approved
- Source calendar or planning tracker
- Change owner role
- Approver role
- Approver name or authorized identifier
- Decision date
- Update cadence
- Conditions

### Decision Wording

Only the approved date wording may be published. Sensitive logistics, venues, contact routes, participant timing, safeguarding arrangements and leader-only preparation details stay out of public view unless separately approved. If the source schedule changes, the website must be updated or the date removed.

## Template 6: Website Content Review Row Update

Required row updates after a decision is recorded:

- Status
- Recommended access treatment
- Required review or approving authority
- Restricted content handling
- Decision evidence location
- Conditions or follow-up owner

Update `Website Content Review` only after the accountable owner records a decision. A pending, deferred or conditional decision must remain visible as pending or conditional. Do not mark a row approved from this template alone.

## Template 7: Publication Handoff

- Handoff ID
- Related WCR rows covered
- Approved content scope summary
- Items withheld or deferred
- Access model dependency status
- Copyright/media dependency status
- Restricted-data check status
- Technical validation status
- Publication authority required
- Rollback or removal owner role
- Evidence locations
- Handoff decision: not ready / ready for publication gate review / ready with conditions

### Publication Handoff Wording

This handoff says the listed content items have review evidence ready for the publication authority to inspect. It does not approve deployment, public sharing, broader access, domain changes, final copy, post-launch announcements or GATE-006 closure. Final publication remains blocked until the accountable authority records a publication decision.

## Tracker Update Rule

Use these templates to update `Website Content Review` decision evidence and conditions, then route approved items into access-model and publication gate review. Update DEC-006, TASK-016, Event Readiness and Launch Gates only after real publication authority records the required decisions.

## Non-Approval Statement

Every template in this pack is a capture aid. It does not replace human review, source-of-truth ownership, copyright permission, data-owner approval, access-model approval, technical validation or final publication authority. Use it to make decisions traceable, not to make decisions on behalf of leaders.
