# Production Website Publication Approval Templates

Use this guide with the native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/1rXpF56LMVwuHwVbqm0KKtuwHBMzY-ljbT-qnLi6BYE0/edit

This package prepares final publication go/no-go capture for GATE-006, DEC-006 and TASK-016. It does not build, deploy, publish, broaden sharing, approve content, approve copyright use, approve access or close GATE-006 by itself.

## When To Use

Use the publication approval templates only after the access model, operating authority, copyright boundary, content scope, restricted-data checks and technical validation evidence are acceptable to the accountable publication authority.

Keep the prototype separate from production unless a specific version, production URL, content scope, access model, rollback owner and publication authority are recorded.

## Publication Boundary

- GATE-005 must be approved before production publication is considered.
- HR-005 operating authority and HR-007 copyright boundary must be accepted or all conditions resolved.
- Public and protected content must be reviewed by the accountable content owner.
- Restricted data must be absent from public pages, exports, screenshots, shared templates and unapproved tools.
- The approved commit or version, production URL, content scope, rollback owner and conditions must be recorded before deployment.
- The prototype remains separate from production unless the publishing authority approves a specific production version.

## Decision Wording Blocks

### Not Approved

Production website publication is not approved. Keep the prototype separate from production, keep Drive and Sheets as the operating source of truth, and record the missing access, authority, content, copyright, data, technical or rollback evidence before review resumes.

### Approved To Build

Production website build work may begin only within the approved content scope, access model and source-of-truth boundary recorded here. This does not approve deployment or public sharing.

### Approved To Deploy

Deployment preparation may begin for the approved commit or version and target URL, subject to the conditions recorded here. This does not approve public announcement or broader sharing unless separately stated by the publishing authority.

### Approved To Publish With Conditions

Publication may proceed only when the listed conditions are complete and the accountable publishing authority records that the conditions are satisfied. Any later content, access, data, domain or tooling change requires a new approval record.

## Publication Evidence Checklist

- DEC-004 records the selected access model and area decisions.
- DEC-006 records the publication decision, authority, date and conditions.
- TASK-015 is complete and TASK-016 is authorized by the approved accountable authority.
- GATE-005, HR-005 and HR-007 are approved or conditions are resolved.
- Approved content scope excludes unreviewed theology, girls programme, safeguarding, medical, legal, copyright or third-party benchmark material.
- Public pages do not include participant, family, leader, reviewer, medical, financial, pastoral or private operational data.
- Protected and restricted areas match the approved access model and Drive/Sheets access groups.
- Source-of-truth Drive links are current and do not expose unapproved locations.
- The technical validation suite is run against the approved version.
- Rollback, monitoring, post-launch review and incident-response ownership are recorded by role.

## Tracker Update Rule

Update DEC-006 first, then TASK-016 and Event Readiness, only after real publication authority records a decision. Update Launch Gates only when all GATE-006 proof exists. Do not deploy, publish, broaden sharing or mark GATE-006 complete from this template alone.
