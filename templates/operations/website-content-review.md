# Website Content Review

## Purpose

Prepare the production website content decision without publishing pages or broadening access. This register lists the proposed website areas, source surfaces, likely access treatment, required human review and restricted-content handling.

## Intended Outcome

Leaders can decide what is public, protected, restricted or withheld before production website work begins. The CSV import template is [website-content-review.csv](website-content-review.csv).

## Operating Rules

- Keep Drive as the source of truth for resources and current operational records.
- Use role names until owner appointment is authorized.
- Do not copy Alpha assets, wording, screenshots, media or branded design elements without explicit permission.
- Do not publish participant records, medical information, banking information, private contact details or unapproved event logistics.
- Treat every row as review routing until the accountable human authority records a decision.

## Required Reviews

Content may require theology, safeguarding, legal, medical, operating authority, girls programme, copyright, data-owner, finance or communications review. The review type in the CSV does not approve the content; it identifies the human gate to complete.

## Evidence Of Completion

A content item is ready for production only when its row records approved access treatment, content scope, reviewer/authority evidence, restricted-content handling and any conditions required before publication.
