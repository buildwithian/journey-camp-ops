# Launch Gate Review Pack

Native Google Doc: https://docs.google.com/document/d/10koplccP__JkX7HWiqq2I2ADNO4BAtCe5H51mGPZUU0/edit

It gathers the live source surfaces, decision order, update sequence and non-approval boundaries in one place so the leadership team can complete real decisions without confusing preparation artifacts for approval evidence.

## Review Session Setup

- Facilitator: Journey Lead or delegated meeting owner.
- Required attendees: accountable programme authority, relevant functional leads and any qualified reviewers needed for gates being discussed.
- Inputs: current tracker rows, review packets, decision templates, pilot evidence and restricted-data approval records.
- Outputs: completed leadership-review output rows, assigned follow-up owner roles, evidence links and explicit gate status changes only where the required human proof exists.

## Operating Rules

- Do not enter real leader names until authorized source review, candidate nomination, backup confirmation and programme-owner approval are complete.
- Do not approve HR-001 through HR-007 unless the qualified reviewer records reviewer name, authority, decision, decision date and any conditions.
- Do not treat scheduled meetings, sent messages or prepared templates as approval.
- Do not copy participant, medical, financial, private contact or restricted personal data into shared trackers.
- Do not broaden sharing, approve website access or publish production pages until GATE-005 and GATE-006 are approved by accountable human authority.

## Recommended Decision Order

1. Confirm the six open launch gates and the exact proof required for each one.
2. Confirm named leadership/authority route before any roster changes.
3. Assign qualified reviewers for HR-001 through HR-007.
4. Schedule and run the pilot cycle, then capture non-sensitive evidence.
5. Review restricted-data placement before replacing synthetic examples.
6. Decide website access model area by area.
7. Decide production publication only after the upstream access, authority, copyright, content and data gates are accepted.

## Gate Prompts

| Gate | Current status | Human decision required |
| --- | --- | --- |
| GATE-001 Appoint owners and governance fields | Not started | Confirm candidates, backup coverage, final authority, availability and training status before any Leader Roster entry. |
| GATE-002 Complete seven human-review gates | Open | Assign qualified reviewers and capture reviewer authority, decision, date and conditions for HR-001 through HR-007. |
| GATE-003 Pilot one real Journey cycle | Not started | Run the pilot with a small leadership team and accept non-sensitive observations, debrief outputs and change-routing evidence. |
| GATE-004 Replace synthetic examples carefully | Review required | Approve storage location, access group, minimum necessary fields, retention and duplicate-copy handling before any migration. |
| GATE-005 Approve website access model | Review required | Choose the access model and decide WAR-001 through WAR-014 before any access change or production work. |
| GATE-006 Build and publish production website | Blocked | Record final go/no-go only after access, authority, copyright, content, restricted-data and technical evidence is accepted. |

## After-Meeting Update Order

1. Update `Leadership Review Outputs` with the meeting output, evidence link, follow-up owner role and status.
2. Update the specific routing/review tab that owns the next action.
3. Update `Tasks`, `Decisions` and `Event Readiness` only where the linked proof exists.
4. Update `Gate Evidence Checklist` before changing any `Launch Gates` status.
5. Update `Launch Gates` last, and only with explicit human approval evidence.

## Non-Approval Statement

This pack prepares leadership review execution only. It does not appoint leaders, approve authority, assign reviewers, complete HR gates, run a pilot, accept pilot findings, authorize restricted-data migration, approve website access, approve publication, broaden sharing, build a production website, publish pages or close any launch gate.
