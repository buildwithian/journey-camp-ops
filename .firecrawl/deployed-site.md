## Activity Playbook & Weather Plan B

Filter Options

Show Plan B

Ice Breaker

Break initial tension and get everyone laughing.

STEPS & LEAD CONSIDERATIONS:

1\. Children introduce fathers to group.
2\. Play Name Game or Two Truths and a Lie.

PLAN B (RAIN / WEATHER CONTINGENCY)

Move under covered veranda / main hall if raining.

Clay Latte

Draw fathers and children together through physical teamwork.

STEPS & LEAD CONSIDERATIONS:

1\. Tie dad and child together at ankles.
2\. Set up obstacle course & dam swim (Lifeguards active on bank).

PLAN B (RAIN / WEATHER CONTINGENCY)

Cancel dam swim if lightning/rain. Move clay building under veranda.

Stokbrood

Quality guided conversational time in relaxed setting.

STEPS & LEAD CONSIDERATIONS:

1\. Lay out dough, stokke, syrup, marshmallows near fire.
2\. Dads and children cook stokbrood and discuss question cards.

PLAN B (RAIN / WEATHER CONTINGENCY)

Move stokbrood cooking to gas stoves under covered gazebo.

Skits & Debrief

Lighthearted fun followed by grounding day experiences.

STEPS & LEAD CONSIDERATIONS:

1\. Groups prepare short funny skit.
2\. Present carving and summarize Saturday takeaways.

PLAN B (RAIN / WEATHER CONTINGENCY)

Fully operable indoors inside main hall.

Malachi

Spiritual reflection on restoration and turning of hearts.

STEPS & LEAD CONSIDERATIONS:

1\. Show YouTube Malachi scripture video on projector.
2\. Sand Demo showing how things naturally drift.

PLAN B (RAIN / WEATHER CONTINGENCY)

Move inside main hall with projector.

Communion

Spiritual reflection, forgiveness, and covenant.

STEPS & LEAD CONSIDERATIONS:

1\. Provide grape juice and bread loaf.
2\. Read 1 Cor 11:23-26. Fathers pray blessing over children.

PLAN B (RAIN / WEATHER CONTINGENCY)

Administer inside main hall with soft lanterns/lighting.

## Water Safety & Dam Protocol

- **DEDICATED LIFEGUARD WATCHING:** NO ONE enters dam unless Lifeguard 1 & 2 are stationed on bank.

- **1:2 ADULT RATIO:** Fathers must actively watch their children in the water zone at all times.

- **LIGHTNING RULE:** Immediate 30-min water evacuation if thunder or lightning occurs within 10km.


## Medical Decision Tree

**Level 1 (Minor):** Scrapes, minor cuts, bee stings → Treat on-site with First Aid kit.


**Level 2 (Moderate):** Deep cut, sprain, allergic reaction → Inform Oversight Lead; drive to Helderberg ER.


**Level 3 (Severe):** Near-drowning, fracture, severe anaphylaxis → Call ER24 immediately; Oversight Lead takes command.
