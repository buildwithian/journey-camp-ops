The Journey Begins Here: Kick Off Camp Overview The Kick Off Camp is a foundational weekend experience designed to ignite connection between fathers and their children. Through intentional shared challenges—like managing a tight food budget, blind packing for each other, and collaborating on camp setup—participants step out of their comfort zones. It is not just about fun; it’s about taking ownership, overcoming obstacles together, and building lasting memories. As leaders, our role is to seamlessly facilitate these experiences so families can focus entirely on relationship-building.

This information is sent directly to the Fathers and Children prior to the camp. It outlines the core challenges they must complete before and during their journey to the campsite.

Budget Challenge

Max Budget: R160 Per Dad & Son/Daughter
  • What to buy: Food for dinner & breakfast, snacks, drinks (venue prohibits alcohol).
  • The Rule: Children oversee spending. Dads may advise, but the final decision is with the child.
  • Receipts must be posted on the group chat before arriving at the camp.
  • Dads are allowed to bring dry braai spices.

Pack Challenge

Without consulting with each other:

  • Child packs an overnight bag for Dad.
  • Dad packs an overnight bag for Child.

What they need to pack (Provided by us):

We provide: Braai drum, grid, kettle, gas cooker, coffee, tea.

They must pack: Tent, sunscreen, hat, sleeping bag, mattress, head torches, spare dirty clothes, blindfold, pen knife, swimming gear, cutlery & plates.
