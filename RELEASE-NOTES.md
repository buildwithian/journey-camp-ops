# Release Notes: v0.1.0

First local release of The Journey Operating System.

## Included

- Privacy-safe source inventory and provenance.
- Current-state audit, MVP scope, roadmap and backlog.
- Foundation, launch, governance and role documentation.
- Reusable session and event pack structures.
- Operational templates and Journey Oversight Tracker.
- Draft safety, safeguarding and quality material.
- Local validation scripts and MkDocs configuration.

## Not Included

- Public publication.
- Custom database or participant management application.
- Final legal, medical, safeguarding, theological, governance, insurance or copyright approval.
