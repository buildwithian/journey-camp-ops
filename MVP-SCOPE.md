# MVP Scope

The v0.1.0 MVP Run Kit includes:

- Foundations and programme map.
- Start-a-Journey guide and launch readiness checklist.
- Role model and responsibility matrix.
- Reusable weekly session and event pack structures.
- Administration, logistics, finance and communications templates.
- Draft safety and safeguarding material marked for expert review.
- Local documentation site configuration.
- Validation scripts for privacy, links, front matter, tracked files and spreadsheet quality.

It does not include a participant management app, database, paid infrastructure, public publishing, or approval of human-only decisions.
